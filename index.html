Our story

One cook, one griddle, a whole lot of heart

Lorena's started the way the best taco spots do: with one person who cooks like she is feeding family. What began as a neighborhood taco truck on Fairview Ave has grown into a little patio kitchen that Boise has fallen for, one plate at a time.

You will often find the owner herself at the window, taking your order and making your food. Customers say it feels like home, and that the owners are some of the nicest people you will meet. That care is the whole recipe.

Que Dios Lo Bendiga · God Bless You
